Frequently Asked Questions

Questions and Solutions

What differentiates Omnitax from a common tax calculator?

Unlike "past generations" that are rigid calculators, Omnitax is a Universal Decision Engine. This means it doesn't just calculate taxes, but audits business rules, reconciles payments, and acts as a system translator (TL).

How does the platform handle Tax Reform changes?

The engine was designed for the daily adaptation required by the next 7 years of legislative transition. It centralizes governance and allows changing calculation rules in seconds, ensuring agility against new technical requirements.

Do I need the IT team to change a calculation rule?

No. Omnitax's great differentiator is No-Code Autonomy. The tax or fiscal user themselves can create logic and formulas in real-time without needing to program or depend on the IT department.

Does Omnitax replace my current ERP (like SAP)?

No, it empowers it. Through our API Gateway, the technology integrates with your current environment (ERP, POS, or TMS), acting as an external brain that processes electronic events in real-time.
